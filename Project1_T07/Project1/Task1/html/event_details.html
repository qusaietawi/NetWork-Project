Al-Ahli Arab Hospital Bombing

A heartbreaking event that shocked the world

Date: October 17, 2023

Location: Al-Ahli Arab Hospital, Gaza

On the evening of October 17, 2023, a devastating airstrike hit the Al-Ahli Arab Hospital in central Gaza, resulting in the tragic death of over 500 civilians. The hospital was serving as a refuge for hundreds of displaced individuals.

  • 500+ casualties
  • Children and elderly among the victims
  • International outrage and calls for investigation

The strike caused mass panic. The world watched in horror as footage of the destruction spread online, showing desperate families searching for survivors under the rubble.
